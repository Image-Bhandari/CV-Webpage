INTRODUCTION

Our assignment 1 of WEB DEVELOPMENT module was to create a web design using HTML and CSS3 properties. The assignment was based on our learning on the first term syllables. The terms like responsive web pages and material design are introduced to us and also are implemented in the project. The assignment was to make a personal CV which could include up to 6 pages and should be operated in mobile phones ( responsive). The CV consists of the following pages:

1. Index : This is the landing page of the CV. This consists of links to the various parts CV.
2.About : In this page you will see a short introduction about who I am, my favorites, hobbies,etc.
3.Contact: through this page you can contact me directly by your emails and can also know my current location.
4.Social: this page consists of various professional website profile of mine and also few social sites through which anyone can easily follow me and know about my searchings.
5.CV : this is the important page of my C.V. this consists of all my basic info, my background, my qualifications, skills, etc.
